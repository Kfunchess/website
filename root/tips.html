Awards

The Ra Ra Award

SPIRIT

Decorations and presentation demonstrate outstanding team / crew effort, enthusiasm, and use of music / sound.

The Eidson Award

LIGHTS

Decorations light up the waterway. Considerations include use of lights, moving lights, laser lights, placement of light, and sophistication level of light usage.

The Sugar Plum Award

EMBRACES THEME

Decorations related to holidays. Considerations include identifiable holiday elements, light and color scheme, and use and placement of identifiable holiday decorations.

The Noel Award

OVERALL

Best use display of all elements, spirit, lights, theme, and holiday.

General Considerations

We hope this list provides inspiring ideas for your holiday themed contribution to this exciting annual event. Remember , with the parade being held in the evening, lights and lit lit decorations will have the greatest impact. Seeing a variety of lights throughout the parade will have a wonderful effect.

Consider icicle lights, rope lights, globe lights, different colored lights, cluster lights ribbon lights, curtain lights and programable lights that twinkle and fade - although it's probably best to limit the number of different types of lights used on any one boat.

Finally, Think Large scale with decorations, as you will be viewed from a distance when in doubt go bigger (as long as you still fit through the canals). Good luck and have fun!
